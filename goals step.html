Goals Step - Anxiety-Reducing Design Principles

Anxiety-Reducing Approach:

  • • "Financial Freedom" vs "Debt Payoff": Positive framing focuses on liberation
  • • "Clear balances" vs "Pay off debt": Gentler terminology around debt
  • • No red "PRIORITY" styling: Removed alarming visual hierarchy
  • • Progress-focused language: "£1,100 of £1,500 cleared" emphasizes achievement
  • • Supportive tips: "Every step forward is progress worth celebrating"
  • • User-controlled prioritization: "Start with what feels most important to you"

Simplified Interface:

  • • Removed Quick Setup: Eliminates overwhelming preset options
  • • Centered Add Buttons: Clear, single action per section
  • • No Timeline/Optimization: Saves complex projections for budget screen
  • • Simple Goal Summary: Just shows allocation totals, not timelines

Enhanced Functionality:

  • • Interest Rate Fields: Optional APR input for debts and savings
  • • Smart Calculations: Interest rates enable better advice later
  • • Pause/Resume Goals: Flexibility without anxiety of deletion
  • • Progress Visualization: Visual bars show completion percentage
  • • Drag & Drop Prioritization: User controls goal ordering

Goal Management States:

  • • Active Goals: Normal styling, counted in allocation
  • • Completed Goals: Green styling with checkmark, celebratory
  • • Paused Goals: Grayed out, £0 allocation, easily resumed
  • • Editing State: Green border when form is expanded

Terminology Improvements:

  • • "Financial Freedom": vs "Debt Payoff"
  • • "Clear outstanding balances": vs "Pay off debts"
  • • "Already paid": vs "Current progress"
  • • "£X remaining": vs "£X owed"
